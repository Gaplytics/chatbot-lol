# Test Modules

Our platform offers a variety of test modules configured by admins and TPOs. Every test is configurable for:
- Toughness Level (Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert)
- No. of Questions
- Time Limit

## Available Modules

### General Modules (All Streams)
- **Reasoning Aptitude**: General aptitude & logical reasoning.
- **Quantitative Aptitude**: Quantitative & analytical skills. (Default: 45 minutes, 60 questions, 1 set)
- **Verbal Aptitude**: Grammar, comprehension, vocabulary.
- **HR Interview**: Behavioural and HR round simulation.

### Tech Modules (B.Tech, M.Tech, BCA, MCA)
- **Coding**: Programming challenges (Python, DSA, SQL).
- **AI/ML RAG**: Multiple choice questions covering DevOps, Cloud, and AI/ML RAG.
- **Tech Interview**: Technical round simulation.

### Non-Tech Modules (BBA, MBA)
- **Functional Test**: Domain-specific multiple choice questions.
- **Domain Interview**: Functional and case study interview rounds.
