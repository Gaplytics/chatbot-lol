# Platform Flow & User Onboarding

## How to Join
1. **Sign Up**: Register on the platform with your details (name, email, stream, year).
2. **Review**: The Gaplytiq team reviews all registrations manually.
3. **Approval**: Once approved, you gain access to the platform.
4. **Dashboard**: You will see a role-based dashboard tailored to your stream (Tech or Non-Tech).

## Roles & Permissions
- **Admin**: Full platform control, user management, configuring tests, and handling approval workflows.
- **TPO (Training & Placement Officer)**: Configures tests for students based on their stream, year, and batch strength.
- **Student**: Accesses courses, assessments, and interview prep on their dashboard.
