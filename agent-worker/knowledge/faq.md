# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How long does the approval process take?**
A: Approvals are done manually by the Gaplytiq team. It usually takes 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I access the coding module if I'm an MBA student?**
A: No, the coding module is strictly for the Tech track (B.Tech, M.Tech, BCA, MCA). MBA students have access to Functional Tests and Domain Interviews.

**Q: Who configures the tests?**
A: The TPO (Training & Placement Officer) configures tests based on your stream, year, and the toughness level required.

**Q: What is Gaplytiq Institute?**
A: It is a community platform designed to provide tailored assessments, interviews, and courses to students to make them industry-ready.
